Adoption Process

There are four requirements to meet when adopting an animal.

  • Everyone in the household must meet the animal. If you are looking to adopt a dog we require a dog meeting to make sure that the animals get along.You must provide proof that your current dog is up to date on shots prior to the meeting.

  • If you rent, we need a renters agreement to show that you are allowed a pet. You must show if you have any restrictions such as how many animals you can own, size limitations, or breed restrictions, if you own your home please bring proof I.E. a tax statement.

  • There is a 24 hours wait on all adoptions, you will be able to pick the pet up the following day. There is a counseling session where we fo over valuable information with you regarding food, training, and care.

  • We require that every adopter must purchase a martingale collar. You may bring your own or purchase on here (ranging from $10-$15). Shelter animals are the most common animals to get loose, a Martingale collar ensures that your new baby gets home safely!

DOG ADOPTION FEES

  • $175.00: For a dog 1 year and over.
  • $275.00: For a dog under 1 year.
  • $275.00: Purebreds without papers (regardless of age).
  • $300.00: Purebreds with papers (regarless of age).

      • Adoption fee includes shots, medical care, deworming, spaying or neautering, microship (registered to the adopted), and rabies (at least 16 weeks old) and heartworm testing (for dogs over 6 months) and flea/tick preventative.

      CAT ADOPTION FEES

      • $40.00: For a cat 1 year and over.
      • $85.00: For a cat under 1 year.
      • $85.00: Exotic cat breds.

          • Adoption fee includes shots, medical care, deworming, spaying or neautering, microship (registered to the adopted), and rabies (cats over 4 months) and FELV/FIV testing.
